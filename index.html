The Science of Vacuum: Air Removal & Compression

The SealTech System uses the SealPump to actively **remove air molecules** from the specialized SealBag. This creates a powerful **vacuum**. The external atmospheric pressure then compresses the bag, significantly **reducing its volume** (Boyle's Law: $P \propto 1/V$ applied externally), locking contents in a compact, protected state.

Simple 5-Step Process

We designed the SealTech System to be incredibly intuitive. Follow these simple steps for quick, easy, and satisfying vacuum sealing.

1

Load

Place your item(s) into the SealBag and lay it flat on a stable surface.

2

Seal

Firmly slide the zipper across the Sealing Track to close the bag completely.

3

Vacuum

Attach the SealPump to the valve and select your desired operating mode.

4

Compress

The pump extracts air; the bag rapidly shrinks. The pump shuts off automatically.

5

Store

Remove the pump and store your now-compact and perfectly protected item.
